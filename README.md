##  Project Overview

This project presents an **Employee Attendance Analysis Dashboard** built entirely in Microsoft Excel. It is designed to help organizations monitor employee attendance patterns, identify absenteeism trends, and generate monthly summary reports — all without any coding.

The dataset and structure are modelled after real-world HR operations in a mid-sized company environment.

---

##  Business Problem

Organizations with 50–200 employees often struggle to track:
- Who is consistently late or absent?
- Which departments have the highest absenteeism?
- How does attendance trend month over month?

This dashboard answers all three questions at a glance.

##  Tools & Features Used

- **Microsoft Excel**
- Pivot Tables — summarise attendance by employee, department, month
- COUNTIF / VLOOKUP formulas — calculate attendance %
- Conditional Formatting — highlight late / absent employees in red
- Bar & Line Charts — visualise monthly attendance trends
- Slicers — filter dashboard by department or month

---

##  Key Insights from the Data

- Overall attendance rate: **87%** across all departments
- Department with lowest attendance: **Operations (81%)**
- Most common reason for absence: **Unplanned leave**
- Month with highest absenteeism: **January**
